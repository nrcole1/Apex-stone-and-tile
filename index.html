About Apex

Local Expertise.
Unmatched Results.

Apex Stone & Tile was founded in Fernandina Beach to serve Nassau County homeowners and businesses who demand more than cookie-cutter results. We're a small, skilled crew — and that means you get a hands-on team who cares about your project from first measurement to final seal.

We specialize in custom installations using natural stone, porcelain, ceramic, and specialty materials. Whether you have a defined vision or just a feeling — we'll help you get there.
